Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your favourite websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices so you can access them on the go.

  • Simple Bookmarking
  • Speedy Searching
  • Easy Sharing

Bookmark in one click

Organize your bookmarks hovewer you like. Our simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete control over how you manage your favorite sites.

Intelligent search

Our powerful search feature will help you find saved sites in no time at all. No need to trawl through all of your bookmarks.

Share your bookmarks

Easily share your bookmarks and collections with other. Create a shareable link that you can send at the click of a button.
